## Summary

I’m a senior software engineer focused on workflow-heavy SaaS products.

Over the last 5 years at Arc XP / The Washington Post, I’ve worked on editorial CMS and subscription platforms where accuracy, trust, and operational reliability matter. I do my best work on systems with complex user workflows, product nuance, and real production constraints.

Before moving into software engineering, I worked as a pharmacist for 6 years. That experience still shapes how I approach product development: careful handling of edge cases, a bias toward clarity, and a strong respect for systems where trust and correctness matter.
